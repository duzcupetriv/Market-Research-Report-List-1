<p><strong>The Landing Gears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Main Landing Gear</li><li>Nose Landing Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The landing gears market comprises two primary types: main landing gear and nose landing gear. Main landing gear, located under the aircraft's fuselage, supports the majority of the aircraft's weight during takeoff and landing, providing stability and shock absorption. In contrast, nose landing gear is situated at the front of the aircraft, guiding directional control during taxiing and supporting the front part of the fuselage. Both types are critical for aircraft safety, performance, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
