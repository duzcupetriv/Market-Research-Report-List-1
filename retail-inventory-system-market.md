<p><p>Retail inventory systems come in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions allow retailers to access inventory management software via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and real-time updates. This model typically reduces upfront costs and allows for easier collaboration. In contrast, on-premises systems involve installing software on local servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. However, they often require higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different business needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
