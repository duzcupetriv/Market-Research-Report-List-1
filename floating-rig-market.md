<p><strong>The Floating Rig Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jackup</li><li>Semi-Submersibles</li><li>Drillships</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floating rig markets consist of several types, primarily jackups, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackups are mobile rigs supported by legs that rest on the seabed, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersibles are anchored vessels that float on the surface, stable in deeper waters and adverse conditions. Drillships are highly versatile ships equipped for drilling operations, often far and deep in the ocean. Each type caters to specific offshore drilling needs, influenced by water depth, location, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
