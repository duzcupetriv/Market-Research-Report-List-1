<p><p>The hollow shaft servo actuator market comprises two primary types: hollow shaft rotary servo actuators and hollow shaft linear servo actuators. Conversely, hollow shaft linear servo actuators provide linear motion, enabling smooth and accurate movement in industrial automation and robotics. Both types leverage their hollow design to streamline installation and reduce overall system complexity, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
