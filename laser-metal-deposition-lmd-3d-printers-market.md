<p><p>The Laser Metal Deposition (LMD) 3D printers market is primarily segmented into two types based on the material feedstock: deposition powder and deposition wire. Deposition powder involves using fine metal powders that are melted by a laser to create intricate structures, benefiting from detailed resolution and material versatility. Deposition wire uses solid metal wires as feedstock, allowing for faster deposition rates and cost efficiency. Both methods are employed in applications like aerospace, automotive, and tooling for their ability to create complex geometries and repair existing components.</p></p>
