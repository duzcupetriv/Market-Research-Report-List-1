<p><p>The calcium ion selective electrodes (ISEs) market is primarily categorized into two types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a solvent polymer membrane that selectively allows calcium ions to pass, offering high sensitivity and fast response times. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride as the main matrix, providing stability and ease of use. Both types are widely used in various applications, including environmental monitoring, food safety, and medical diagnostics, due to their reliable ion detection capabilities.</p></p>
