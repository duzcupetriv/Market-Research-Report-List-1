<p><strong>The Online Viscometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibration Online Viscometer</li><li>Rotary Online Viscometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online viscometer market includes various types that measure the viscosity of fluids in real-time. Vibration online viscometers utilize oscillating elements to determine viscosity through the fluid’s resistance to vibration, providing quick and accurate measurements. On the other hand, rotary online viscometers measure viscosity by rotating a spindle within the fluid, generating torque that correlates with viscosity levels. Both types are essential in industries like oil and gas, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals for optimizing processes and ensuring product quality.</p></p>
