<p><p>Class II Biological Safety Cabinets are essential for working with materials that require protection for both the user and the environment. Type A2 cabinets recirculate 70% of the air within the cabinet while exhausting 30% back to the environment, suitable for low-risk procedures. Type B2 cabinets, on the other hand, provide a direct exhaust to the outside, ensuring the highest level of containment for handling hazardous materials. Both types are critical in laboratories, research facilities, and healthcare settings, catering to varying safety needs.</p></p>
