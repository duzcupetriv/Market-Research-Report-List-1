<p><p>The multilayer paper bags market includes several types designed for various applications. Sewn open mouth bags are durable and suited for bulk products. Pinch bottom bags offer easy closure and are typically used for products like grains. Self-opening satchels provide quick access and convenience, ideal for packaging items like snacks and pet foods.</p></p>
