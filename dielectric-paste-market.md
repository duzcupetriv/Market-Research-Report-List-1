<p><strong>The Dielectric Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Curing Paste</li><li>Sintered Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dielectric paste is categorized into two main types: curing paste and sintered paste. Curing paste is primarily used for applications requiring thermal and electrical insulation, curing upon application to form a stable bond that resists moisture and contamination. Conversely, sintered paste involves a powder that, when heated, forms a solid mass, making it suitable for high-temperature applications. Both types play critical roles in electronics and semiconductor industries, enhancing device performance and reliability through their unique properties.</p></p>
