<p><p>The Mechanical Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) equipment market primarily features two types: Spiral IQF and Tunnel IQF systems. Spiral IQF utilizes a continuous, spiral conveyor that optimizes space and minimizes freezing time, making it ideal for food items like vegetables and seafood. In contrast, Tunnel IQF employs a linear, tunnel-shaped freezing chamber where products move through chilled air, suitable for high-volume processing. Both systems enhance food preservation and texture by freezing items quickly and uniformly.</p></p>
