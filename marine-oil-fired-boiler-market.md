<p><strong>The Marine Oil-fired Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Boilers</li><li>High Pressure Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Oil-fired Boiler market is categorized into two main types: low pressure and high pressure boilers. Low pressure boilers operate at lower steam pressures, making them suitable for processes requiring bulk steam at a controlled rate, primarily for heating purposes. In contrast, high pressure boilers generate steam at elevated pressures, essential for powering steam turbines and various marine operations requiring efficient energy generation. The choice between these types depends on operational requirements, efficiency, and regulatory compliance in marine environments.</p></p>
