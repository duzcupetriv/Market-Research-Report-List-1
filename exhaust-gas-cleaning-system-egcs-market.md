<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Mixed Waste Gas Purification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>An Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) is designed to reduce sulfur emissions from marine vessels by treating exhaust gases. The market is segmented into three types: </p><p>1. **Open Loop EGCS**: Utilizes seawater for scrubbing exhaust gases, discharging treated water back into the ocean.</p><p>2. **Closed Loop EGCS**: Employs freshwater, recycling it after treatment, thus preventing environmental discharge.</p><p>3. **Mixed Waste Gas Purification System**: Combines both open and closed-loop methods, adapting to varying operational circumstances for efficient emissions control.</p></p>
