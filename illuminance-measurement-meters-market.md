<p><strong>The Illuminance Measurement Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Illuminance Photometers"</li><li>"Illuminance Colorimeters"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The illuminance measurement meters market encompasses two primary types: illuminance photometers and illuminance colorimeters. Illuminance photometers measure the intensity of light perceived by the human eye, providing readings in lux or foot-candles to assess lighting conditions. In contrast, illuminance colorimeters analyze the color properties of light, measuring parameters like color temperature and chromaticity. Both devices are essential for ensuring appropriate lighting levels in various applications, from architectural design to photography, enhancing visual comfort and accuracy in lighting assessments.</p></p>
