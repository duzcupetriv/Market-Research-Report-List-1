<p><p>Transparent Evaporated Barrier Packaging Film is primarily categorized into two types: AlOx and SiOx types. AlOx films utilize aluminum oxide to provide excellent barrier properties against moisture and oxygen, making them suitable for food and pharmaceutical applications. In contrast, SiOx films employ silicon oxide, offering superior transparency and flexibility while maintaining high barrier performance. Both types enhance product shelf life and are essential in industries requiring effective packaging solutions while minimizing environmental impact through thinner film options.</p></p>
