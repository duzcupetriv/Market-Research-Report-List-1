<p><p>The Upper Extremities Fixation Product Market includes various internal and external fixation types. Internal fixation comprises plates, screws, nails, and other devices used to stabilize bone fractures within the body. Plates offer broad support for fractures, while screws provide targeted stabilization. Nails are often used for long bone fixation, and other methods include intramedullary options. External fixation involves devices applied outside the body to stabilize fractures through pins or screws, allowing for mobility and support during recovery.</p></p>
