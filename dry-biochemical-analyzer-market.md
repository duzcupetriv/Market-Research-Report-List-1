<p><p>The dry biochemical analyzer market is divided into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic biochemical analyzers. Semi-automatic analyzers require some manual intervention for sample preparation and data interpretation, making them suitable for smaller laboratories with limited throughput needs. In contrast, automatic biochemical analyzers operate independently, processing samples and delivering results with minimal human involvement, ideal for high-throughput settings. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in biochemical testing, catering to varying laboratory requirements and operational capacities.</p></p>
