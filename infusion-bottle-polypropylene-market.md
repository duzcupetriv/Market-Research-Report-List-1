<p><strong>The Infusion Bottle Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP-H</li><li>PP-R</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infusion bottle polypropylene market is primarily segmented into two types: PP-H (Homopolymer) and PP-R (Random Copolymer). PP-H is known for its high rigidity and strength, making it suitable for applications requiring structural integrity. In contrast, PP-R offers better impact resistance and flexibility, often used in medical applications that require sterility and durability. Both types provide excellent chemical resistance and are increasingly favored in the healthcare sector for manufacturing safe, reliable infusion bottles.</p></p>
