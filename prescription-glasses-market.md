<p><p>The prescription glasses market primarily offers two types: single vision and progressive glasses. Single vision prescription glasses are designed for correcting a specific type of vision issue, such as nearsightedness or farsightedness, providing clear vision at one distance. In contrast, progressive prescription glasses feature a gradual lens design that accommodates multiple vision ranges—near, intermediate, and far—without visible lines, making them versatile for users with presbyopia. Both types cater to various visual needs and enhance daily activities.</p></p>
