<p><p>In the oil and gas processing sector, seals are essential for preventing leaks and maintaining system integrity. The single seal type consists of a single mechanical barrier, ideal for lower pressure and less hazardous applications. Conversely, double seals utilize two barriers, providing enhanced safety and reliability in high-pressure or hazardous environments. The double seal design also features an intermediate space that can detect leaks, making it suitable for critical applications, where minimizing the risk of contamination or accidents is paramount.</p></p>
