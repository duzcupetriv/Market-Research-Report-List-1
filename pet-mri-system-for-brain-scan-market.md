<p><ul><li>Traditional PET-MRI Systems</li><li>Helium-free PET-MRI Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET-MRI system for brain scans is categorized into two main types: traditional PET-MRI systems and helium-free PET-MRI systems. Traditional systems utilize liquid helium for cooling the MRI magnets, which can lead to resource constraints and higher operational costs due to helium shortages. In contrast, helium-free PET-MRI systems use alternative cooling technologies, making them more sustainable and cost-effective. Both types enable simultaneous imaging, enhancing diagnostic capabilities for neurological conditions by providing detailed metabolic and structural information.</p></p>
