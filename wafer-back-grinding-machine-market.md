<p><ul><li>Wafer Edge Grinding Machine</li><li>Wafer Surface Grinding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer back-grinding machine market encompasses two primary types: wafer edge grinding machines and wafer surface grinding machines. Wafer edge grinding machines focus on processing the edges of wafers to enhance their dimensional accuracy and eliminate defects, ensuring compatibility in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, wafer surface grinding machines target the overall surface quality, reducing wafer thickness and improving surface finish for better performance. Both types are essential for optimizing wafer preparation in semiconductor production.</p></p>
